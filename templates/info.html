How to Use AI Chess

Welcome to AI Chess! Here, you play chess against a powerful AI opponent powered by Stockfish. The platform is designed to help you improve your chess skills, analyze your games, and track your progress over time.

Game Modes & Customization

  • Player vs AI: Every game is played against the AI. You can choose to play as White or Black.
  • Difficulty Levels: Select from Easy, Medium, or Hard AI difficulty before each game. The AI adapts its strength and depth of calculation accordingly.
  • Move Input: Click on the board to select and move pieces. Pawn promotions are supported with a choice of piece.
  • Custom Board Setup: Before starting a game, you can set up the board in any position you like to practice openings, endgames, or specific scenarios.

Scoring System

  • Move Scoring: Every move you make is evaluated by the AI and given a score from 0 to 10, where 10 is the best possible move and 0 is a blunder.
  • Score Calculation: The score reflects how close your move is to the optimal move in the position, based on the AI's analysis.
  • Game Analysis: After each game, you can view a graph of your move scores, see your best and worst moves, and get personalized insights.

Statistics & Insights

  • Performance Tracking: Your stats page shows your win/loss/draw record, rating, and average move quality.
  • Game Review: After each game, review your moves, see which were brilliant or blunders, and get suggestions for improvement.
  • Sharing: Share your stats and game analysis with friends directly from the platform.

How to Get Started

  1. Click Play to start a new game against the AI.
  2. Choose your color and difficulty.
  3. Make your moves by clicking on the board.
  4. After the game, view your stats and analysis on the Stats page.

Tips

  • Try different difficulties to challenge yourself.
  • Review your move scores to identify areas for improvement.
  • Use the stats and insights to track your progress over time.
